ABOUT HIS MERCY APOSTOLIC CHURCH

Our Story

His Mercy Apostolic Church evolved from a prayer program, God of All Flesh under the leadership of Pastor and Pastor(Mrs) Israel Olawale Adeigbe as the founders. Having served in R.C.C.G for well over two decades with an official release by Daddy Adeboye, Pastor Israel Adeigbe was led to pursue the apostolic mandate of Prayers and the Word as the Lord has sent him. H.M.A commenced services on 27th February, 2021 and the headquarters is located at 5, Victoria Onipede Close, Agnes Adeniran, Riskat Majaro Street, U/Turn Bus Stop, Abule- Egba, Lagos, Nigeria.

Our Vision

  1. To make heaven.
  2. To evangelize the world.
  3. To rekindle the fire of prayers to God, in the lives of believers.

Our Mission

  1. To achieve (1) above, holiness will be our lifestyle.
  2. To achieve (2) above, we shall be committed to winning a soul per day for Christ by His grace.
  3. To achieve vision (3) above, the Bible will be our foundation for prayers.

  • About Our Beliefs

    • We believe in the Bible as the living word of God.
    2Timothy 3:16-17.

  • About God

    • We believe in one God; the Maker of all creatures.
    Isaiah 43:10-11.

  • About Jesus

    • We believe that Jesus is the Son of God and also the Saviour of the world. Whoever believes in Him shall be saved.
    John 1:1-14, Galatians 4:4-5.

  • About the Holy Spirit

    • We believe that the Holy Spirit is the third in the Godhead. He lives in everyone that has given their lives to Jesus Christ.
    Genesis 1:1-3, Mark 1:10. Act 1:8.

  • About the Trinity

    • We believe God the Father, God the Son and God the Holy Spirit are one with distinct manifestations. They are the Trinity.
    Genesis 1:1-3, Matthew 18:19. 1 John 5:7.

  • About Man

    • We believe that man is an express image of God. Man needs God to remain eternally relevant. This is only possible through the salvation plan or provision made by God.
    Colossians 1:21-22.

  • About Salvation

    • The blood of Jesus is the only provision for salvation. It guarantees forgiveness of sins and redemption when we have faith in it.
    Ephesians 2:8-10, John 1:12.

  • About Water Baptism

    • We believe in water baptism by immersion into a flowing river. This must be done in the name of God the Father, Son and the Holy Ghost. This should be done by every believer in Christ.
    Matthew 28:19, Act 8:27-39.

  • About Holy Ghost Baptism

    • We believe all believers in Christ Jesus are expected to receive the promise of the Father.i. e Holy Spirit baptism. It is God's promise to believers who wait on Him.
    Luke 24:49, Act 1: 4-5, Act 2:4.

  • About Prayers

    • We believe in Bible based prayers through the leading of the Holy Spirit. All prayers must be done in the name of Jesus.
    Psalm 50:15, Roman 8:26, James 1:6-8.

  • About Restitution

    • We believe that restitution is a mark of true repentance, but it must be done according to the leading of the Lord.
    Proverbs 28:13, Luke 19:8.

  • About Marriage

    • We believe that all members who are willing to get married could do so in the church and according to the doctrines of the Bible. We believe in one man and one wife.
    Matthew 19:9, Roman 7:2-3.

  • About Holy Communion

    • We believe in gathering together regularly to share the bread( Flesh of Christ) and the wine( Blood of Christ).
    1 Corinthians 11: 23-30, Matthew 26:26-29.

  • About the Church.

    • We believe in the gathering of our members regularly to worship God.
    Act 14:23, Ephesians 1:22.

  • About Resurrection of the Dead and Second Coming of Christ.

    • We believe the dead in Christ shall resurrect on the last day at the sound of the last trumpet. The dead in Christ will rise first, then we who are alive in Him shall be caught up in the cloud to meet our Lord.
    Act 24:15, Philippians 3:20-21, 1Thessalonians 4:16-17.
