Our Church Programs

We have various spirit-filled programs designed to uplift and impact lives in our community.

  • Sunday Services
  • God of All Flesh
  • Chaplaincy
  • Feed My Lambs
  • Bible College

Sunday Services

Every Sunday, we gather as a family of believers to worship, learn, and fellowship. Our services are filled with vibrant praise, heartfelt prayers, and life-transforming messages. Whether you are new in faith or a long-time believer, you will find a place of love, encouragement, and growth in our church.

God of All Flesh

God of All Flesh is a powerful prayer gathering that happens on every Tuesday. It is a time of intense worship, prophetic declarations, and divine encounters. We believe in the unlimited power of God to change lives, heal the sick, and grant breakthroughs. Come and experience the move of God in your life!

Chaplaincy

Our Chaplaincy program is dedicated to providing spiritual guidance and support in different spheres of life, including schools, hospitals, and correctional facilities. Through this initiative, we extend the love of Christ beyond the church walls, ministering to those in need of hope, counseling, and encouragement.

Feed My Lambs

Feed My Lambs is our community outreach program where we demonstrate God's love through acts of kindness. Our pastor and church members actively provide food and essential supplies to the less privileged. It is our mission to care for both the spiritual and physical well-being of those in our community.

Bible College

Our Bible College is dedicated to equipping believers with in-depth biblical knowledge and spiritual growth. Whether you are preparing for ministry, seeking to deepen your faith, or simply want to understand God's Word better, our courses provide a strong foundation in theology, discipleship, and Christian leadership.
